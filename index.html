Cyber-Physical Systems & Agentic AI Architecture

Your constant for a system in flux.

Flux Constant Labs works at the intersection of cyber-physical systems and agentic AI — building the edge architecture that keeps modern compute safe within physical infrastructure, and the training environments that prepare people to defend it.

Practice Areas

Cyber-Physical Infrastructure

Architecting least-privilege data pipelines, proxy networks, and edge deployments that bridge physical hardware with modern compute. We engineer the physical and digital routing for environments where failure is not an acceptable outcome.

Agentic AI Pipelines

Inspectable, local-first LLM architectures for autonomous systems. We design multi-agent workflows utilizing edge inference, vector databases, and stateful memory — ensuring your AI's context can be strictly routed, checked, and controlled offline.

Exercise Environments

Designing full-scale, hardware-in-the-loop testbeds that support technical research and mission assurance validation alike. From emulated cyber ranges to physical exercise infrastructure, we build the environments where systems are tested and readiness is proven, sustained through ongoing drills as teams grow, evolve, and turn over.

Experiential Learning

Developing targeted curricula and syllabi mapped directly to your organization's priority learning objectives. We design mission rehearsal exercises and comprehensive training materials that drive real workforce development, not just compliance training.
